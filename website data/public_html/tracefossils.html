Trace Fossils

This page is where we will compile a glossary of ichnotaxa, presented with color images and descriptions. They will be listed here by ichnotaxa name and labeled with the associated tracemaker name for convenience. Check back, as we will continue to update the glossary with new ichnotaxa and new images. If you are interested in videos of trace fossils, trace making behavior, or the research performed in our lab, check the videos section of our website.

Entelodontipus: Casanovas-Cladellas and Santafe-Llopis, 1982

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Two digit track with hoof impressions equal in length. Hooves are subparallel to subelliptical in outline and are broadest medially. Hooves are parallel to trackway.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Artiodactyla animal, likely the suiform entelodontids.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Oligocene

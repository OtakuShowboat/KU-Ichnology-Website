Falcatipes: Sarjeant and Langston, 1994

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A small, semidigitigrade track with large phalangeal pads. The presumed pes has digits II–V and they all curve inward. The presumed manus is smaller and has digits I–V; digit I is reduced and digits I–IV curve inward. All digits have claw impressions with digits I–IV of the manus and digits II–IV of the pes curving inward.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Largely unknown though the best prediction would be a miacid.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Eocene

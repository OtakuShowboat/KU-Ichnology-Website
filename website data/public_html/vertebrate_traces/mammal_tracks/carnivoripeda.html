Carnivoripeda: Kordos, 1985

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tracks with five digits, all ending in distinct claw impressions. The tracks are about as wide as they are tall. The toe impressions are droplet-shaped and end in an apex.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Carnivora

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Miocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Description is the same for the ichnospecies Carnivoripeda nogradensis.

Thulitheripus: Luthje et al., 2010

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Pentadactyl tracks with short, anterior directed digits. Digits IV and III are the longest with the lateral digits decreasing in length. Digit impressions are triangular. The pes is almost symmetrical, trapezoidal outline and a triangular heel. Pes is about twice the size of manus. Trackway is narrow.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: A pantodont mammal, very likely a Titanoideidae.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Paleocene

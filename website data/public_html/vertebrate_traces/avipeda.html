Avipeda: Vialov, 1965

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): Passeripedia

DESCRIPTION: An incumbent anisodactyl track with three, short and thick digits each ending with noticeable claw impressions. Digit III is less than 25% longer than digits II and IV. Angle of divarication is about 95 degrees or less. Digits converge closely and there is a lack of webbing.

Schyromorphipus: Sarjeant and Langston, 1994

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A small, plantigrade impression with claw impressions on all digits. Pes is slightly larger than manus. The pes is similar in structure to the manus with the exception that the hallux diverges less than 15 degrees from the manus and it has a long heel.

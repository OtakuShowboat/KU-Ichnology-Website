Tatarornipes: Lockley et al., 2012

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: An incumbent anisodactyl track with about a wide (greater than 90 degrees) angle of divarication. All three digits have thick impressions and are wider proximally than distally. Trackway is narrow, has little inward rotation and a long step.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environment

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Either small dinosaurs or large birds.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

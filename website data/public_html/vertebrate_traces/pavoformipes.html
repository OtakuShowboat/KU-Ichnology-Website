Pavoformipes: Lockley and Delgado, 2007

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A tridactyl or tetradactyl, subsymmetrical track that is wide as it is long. The hallux, digit I, is postero-medially directed. The track has a rounded heel pad.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: A Meleagridae, turkey, bird or relative.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Miocene

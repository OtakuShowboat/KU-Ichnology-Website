Ganasauripus: Valentini et al., 2007

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Pentadactyl footprints of a quadrupedal reptile. Digits increase in length from I to IV. Ectaxonic pes, mesaxonic manus. Pes turned outward with respect to the midline, manus turned inward and more deeply impressed than the pes. Manus internal to the pes. The pes and the manus are semiplantigrade to plantigrade. Divarication of digits IV-V may encompass 90Â° both in the manus and in the pes. Digits I-IV of the pes are slightly bent and coalesce at the base, which is narrow. Slightly sinusoidal tail-drag marks.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Quadrupedal reptile.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Permian

Pteraichnus: Stokes 1957

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Quadruped tracks with manus impressions the same axis or nearly so to the pes prints. Pes prints anteriorly located to the manus prints. Pes prints are elongated, subtriangular, plantigrade and have four digits. Digit II and III are slightly longer than digits I and IV. All digit impressions have claw impressions. Manus has three digits and is elongated, asymmetrical and digitigrade. Digit I commonly has claw impression and is anteriorly located. Digit III is posteriorly located. Digit II and III rarely have claw impressions. In well preserved manus and pes impressions, phalanges can be observed.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Pterasaurian

Cincosaurus: Aldrich, 1930

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Quadrupedal trackway with a pentadactyl manus and pes. Digit I–IV increase in length with digit V smaller in size, positioned back and laterally. The manus is slightly smaller than the pes is and is directed medially. Digit III of the pes is parallel to the midline. The pace angulation (of the manus) is 100–120°.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: mammal-like reptile (e.g., Pelycosaur), amphibian

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Pennsylvanian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Schneck and Fritz, 1985

Borealosuchipus: Erikson, 2005

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Digits I–IV of the right manus and pes are preserved (but poorly), measurements were unable to be taken. The largest digit on the manus is digit III, the length of the manus is 9.0 cm and the width is 8.5 cm. The imprint of the pes is larger with a length of 11.5 cm and widths of 11.0 cm. Drag marks produced by the animal’s abdomen are present.

BEHAVIOR(S): Cubichnia (resting (e.g., possibly basking))

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Crocodilian

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Paleocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Poorly preserved specimens, may be synonomized in the future.

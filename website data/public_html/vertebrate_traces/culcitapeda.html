Culcitapeda: Sarjeant and Reynolds, 2001

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: An incumbent anisodactyl track with a strong presence of webbing. A circular outline of the posterior side of the track. Digit III extends longer posteriorly and anteriorly compared to digits II and IV.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environments

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Shoreline birds, possibly made by relatives of the flamingo.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Miocene

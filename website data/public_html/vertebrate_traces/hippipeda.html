Hippipeda: Vialov, 1966

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tracks of a large, single digit Track is hemispherical to hemiellipsoidal in outline and can have a frog. Narrow trackway with a long stride and pace.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Perissodactyla animals, particularly those belonging to Equid animals.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Miocene-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Sarjeant and Reynolds, 1999. Can also be found in Remeika, 1999.

REMARKS: The large, central digit is in the form of a hoof. Track can have the two lateral digits (II and IV) be impressed during a fast gait but most of the time it will only be the much larger central impression of digit III.

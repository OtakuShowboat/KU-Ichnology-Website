Broomichnium: Kuhn, 1958

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small, bilaterally symmetrical impression made up of two pairs of thin curvilinear or linear imprints. Each imprint are almost identical. Outer pair nests the inner pair. Outer imprints have a greater angle of divarication than inner imprints. Inner imprints may be parallel or diverge from the median.

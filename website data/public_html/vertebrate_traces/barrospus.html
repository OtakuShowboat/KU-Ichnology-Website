Barrosopus: Coria et al., 2002

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A small, incumbent anisodactyl track with an angle of divarication between digits II–IV between 100–120 degrees. The digit II impression is not attached to the rest of the track unlike digits III and IV. The trackway has a long stride.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environment

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Birds, possibly very small dinosaurs

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Cretaceous

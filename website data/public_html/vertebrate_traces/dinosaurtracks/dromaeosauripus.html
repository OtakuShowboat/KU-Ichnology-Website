Dromaeosauripus: Kim et al., 2008

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A didactyl track about 15 cm long and 9 cm wide. Digit impressions are slender with a very narrow angle of divarication (5–10 degrees) between digits III–IV. Digit IV is slightly shorter than digit III. Well preserved and distinct digital pad and sharp claw impressions. Pes impressions are nearly parallel to trackway axis.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Deinonychosauria dinosaurs

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Kim et al., 2012

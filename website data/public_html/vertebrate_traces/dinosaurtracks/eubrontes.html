Eubrontes: Hitchcock, 1845

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): Jinlijingpus, Paracoelurosaurichnus, Youngichnus

DESCRIPTION: Large (>25 cm) tridactyl pes of a bipedal dinosaurian. The pes is wide with a short digit III and a rarely preserved hallux. The divarication of the the digits is 25–40°.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: therapod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic–Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Olsen et al., 1998. Lucas et al., 2006

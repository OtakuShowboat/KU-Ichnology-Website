Siamopodus: Lockley et al., 2006

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl track that is small to medium in size. Digits are slender and the heel is bilobed and sub-symmetric. The space between digits III-IV is anteriorly located to the space between digits II-III.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Small to medium-sized therapod dinosaurs

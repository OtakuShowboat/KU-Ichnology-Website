Delatorrichnus: Casamiquela, 1964

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Trackway has symmetrical pes and manus impressions. Manus impressions are in contact or are located medially to the pes impressions. Manus and pes impressions are longer than wide. The pes impression is almost twice the size of the manus impression. Manus is either tridactyl or tetradactyl. The digits are subparallel and are rhomboid-like in outline. Pes is tridactyl with robust and low angle of divarication digit impressions. Impressions lack digital pads. Digit III is the longest. Trackway is wide with the pes and manus having a high pace angulation.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Ornithischian dinosaur

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by de Valais, 2011

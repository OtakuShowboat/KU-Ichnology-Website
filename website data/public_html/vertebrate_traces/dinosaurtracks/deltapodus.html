Deltapodus: Whyte and Romano, 1994

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: The manus’ outline is broadly crescent–shaped and is anteriorly convex. The manus occasionally has a medially directed pollex impressions. The pes’ outline is generally oval or sub-triangular. The pes’ internal side is slightly concave with three short, highly rounded digit impressions. The internal trackway is moderately wide (about 0.1–0.3 meters).

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Likely a stegosaurian dinosaur such as Dacentrurus.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Cobos et al., 2010

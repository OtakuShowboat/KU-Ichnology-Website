Anchisauripus: Lull, 1904

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Medium-sized tridactyl track. Digit III is relatively longer than Eubrontes but shorter than Grallator. Length of track is about twice as long as its width. Hallux (digit I) is rarely preserved. Angle of divarication between 20-35 degrees.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapod dinosaurs

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Early Jurassic

REMARKS: Information taken from Olsen et al., 1998.

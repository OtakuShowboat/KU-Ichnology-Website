Grallator: Hitchcock, 1858

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): Chongqingpus, Chuanchengpus

DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl pes imprint of a bipedal dinosaurian. The pes is directed foreward and digits II–IV are persevered with digit III being the longest. The width of the pes is greater than the length and the angle of divarication between the preserved digits is 18–37°.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic–Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Olsen et al., 1998

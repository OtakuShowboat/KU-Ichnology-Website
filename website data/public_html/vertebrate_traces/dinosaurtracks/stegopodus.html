Stegopodus: Lockley and Hunt, 1998

JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Medium to large, pes tridactyl tracks. Tracks are digitigrade semibiped. Asymmetrical pes with a posterolaterally located proximal pad. Pes digits are broad, very short and have a wide angle of divarication. Pes digits also only project just beyond the hypex. Manus is tetradactyl and entaxonic.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Stegosaurian

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Gierlinski and Sabath, 2008

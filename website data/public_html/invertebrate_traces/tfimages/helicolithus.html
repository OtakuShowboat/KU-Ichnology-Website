Helicolithus: Azpeitia-Moros, 1933

DESCRIPTION: Graphoglyptid burrow with large first order meanders; smaller undulations from a helicoidal spiral; screw direction changes with every turn of the large meanders.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep-marine

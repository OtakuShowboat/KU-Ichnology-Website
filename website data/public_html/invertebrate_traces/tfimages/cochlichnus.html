Cochlichnus: Hitchcock, 1858

DESCRIPTION: Small sinuous or meandering smooth horizontal trace. Trace resembles a sine curve.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Shallow to deep marine, aquatic continetal. In aquatic continental, made when the water table is at or near the sediment-water-air interface.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Aquatic annelids, insect larvae, nematodes.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Ediacaran-recent.

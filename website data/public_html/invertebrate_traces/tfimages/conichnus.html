Conichnus : Myannil, 1996

DESCRIPTION: Vertically orientated fillings of cone-shaped hollows, circular in cross section with a rounded bottom.

BEHAVIOR(S): Dwelling, suspension feeding.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine to terrestrial, lacustrine.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Anemone, clams.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Ordovician-recent.

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Frey and Howard, 1981)

Rosselia : (Dahmer, 1937)

DESCRIPTION: Conical to irregular, funnel-shaped, vertical, concentric burrow with a central cylindrical pencil-thick tube.

BEHAVIOR(S): Domichnia, fodinichnia; Dwelling and feeding burrows of a filter- or suspension-feeding organism.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Found in shallow marine sublittoral and deep-water flysch deposits.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Annelid worms, arthropods, sea anemones.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian-recent

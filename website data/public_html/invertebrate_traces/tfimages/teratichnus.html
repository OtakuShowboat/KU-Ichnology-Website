Teratichnus: Miller, 1880

DESCRIPTION: Trackways of numerous narrow impressions, somewhat rarely bifurcated, asymmetrical sets, can be arranged in oblique sets, can be crowded together on the right side of the trail and separated on the left, may be separable into rows.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: marine

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Trilobites or Cephalopods

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Ordovician-Silurian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Miller, 1880; Miller, 1889

Chubutolithes: Ihering, 1922

DESCRIPTION: Nest structure consisting of small, thin cells arranged around a central cylinder. May contain shared walls.

BEHAVIOR(S): Reproduction

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Alluvial: floodplain to deltaic

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Wasps (mud-daubers)

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Bown and Ratcliffe, 1988; Genise and Cladera, 2004

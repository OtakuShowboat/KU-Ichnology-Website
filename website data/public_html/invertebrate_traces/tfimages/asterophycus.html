Asterophycus: Lesquereux, 1876

DESCRIPTION: Star-shaped trace with 3–7 rays or lobes radiating outward from a vertical, central tube. Trace tapers in thickness near outer edge of trace.

BEHAVIOR(S): Feeding or possible dwelling.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine; shoreface to shelf

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Worms or crustaceans

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Carboniferous–Pennsylvanian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Orlowski and Ylińska, 2002

Multina : Orlowski, 1968

DESCRIPTION: Endichnial, horizontal, irregular net of thin unlined or thinly lined strings (0.2-0.9mm diameter), filled with different colored media than host media. Nets are irregular both in shape and size, 1-5mm wide. Strings do branch, but are distributed on different layers causing overcrossings (Kotlarczyk and Uchman, 2012).

BEHAVIOR(S): Grazing trail.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep Marine (Uchman, 1998).

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Unknown

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Eocene?-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Uchman, 1998; Kotlarczyk and Uchman, 2012

Gordia : Emmons, 1844

DESCRIPTION: Long, slender, smooth trails of uniform thickness throughout; mostly bent but not meandering.

BEHAVIOR(S): Grazing and feeding trace.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine to continental aquatic.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: vermiform organisms, insect larvae, millipedes, gastropods.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Precambrian-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Fillion and Pickerill, 1990. Buatois and Mangano, 2007; Uchman, et al., 2009.

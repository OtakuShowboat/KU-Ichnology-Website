Alacranichnus: Lucas et al., 2013

DESCRIPTION: Trace composed of three divisions; broad anterior portion; broad straight midsection; curved and tapered posterior section.

BEHAVIOR(S): Cubichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Coastal plain

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Arthropod; scorpion

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Permian

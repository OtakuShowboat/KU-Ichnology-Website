Taphrhelminthopsis: Sacco, 1888

DESCRIPTION: Bilobate trails, meandering, similar to Scolicia De Quatrefages but with meanders or tightly coiled spirals.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: marine flysch deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Echinoderms, worms?

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Tertiary

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Smith and Crimes, 1983, Wetzel and Bromley, 1996.

REMARKS: Taphrhelminthopsis may be considered a preservational variant of Scolicia.

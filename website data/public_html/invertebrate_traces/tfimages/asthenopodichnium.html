Asthenopodichnium: Thenius, 1979

DESCRIPTION: Oblong borings that are over 2 cm long and over 0.5 cm wide

BEHAVIOR(S): Polychresichnia; dwelling, reproduction

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Forested, temperate continental environments, lacustrine.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Mayflies

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Permian-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Emended: Uchman et al., 2007)(Moran et al., 2010)

REMARKS: Uchman et al., 2007 suggested an emendment that included bone as a medium and spreiten structures.

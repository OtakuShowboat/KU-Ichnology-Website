Paleoscotylus Walker, 1938

DESCRIPTION: Intersecting networks of straight millimeter-scale (~5 mm) borings in multiple directions.

BEHAVIOR(S): Habitation, reproduction. Tracemakers also feed on the moisture within live, woody medium.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Fully continental. Relatively temperate, forested environments.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Beetles (adult and larvae)

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Carboniferous-recent

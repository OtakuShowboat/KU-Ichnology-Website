Lockeia : James, 1879

DESCRIPTION: Almond-shaped oblong bodies preserved in convex hyporelief tapering at both ends. Surface commonly smooth.

BEHAVIOR(S): Resting trace.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Any aquatic environment where bivalves can be found from marine to brackish water to freshwater fluvial and lacustrine.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Bivalves.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Ordovician-recent.

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Maples and West, 1989)

Teichichnus : Seilacher, 1955

DESCRIPTION: A vertical stack of horizontally-oriented burrows with spreiten.

BEHAVIOR(S): Domichnia, Fodinichnia, Equilibrichnia?; Dwelling and feeding burrow of a deposit feeder. Trace could possibly be an equilibrium structure.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Found in lower marine shorefaces, intertidal settings, lagoons, deep-marine fans and abyssal plains, and brackish water settings like marine estuaries.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Annelid worms

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Häntzschel, 1975)(Fillion and Pickerill, 1990)(Mørk and Bromley, 2008)

Octopodichnus : Gilmore, 1927

DESCRIPTION: Trace of small elliptic depressions on bedding surface. Arranged in repeating groups of two anterior tracks and two posterior tracks.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion trace of organism walking across medium surface.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine to continental. Continental environments include eolian.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Crustaceans (marine). Eight legged arachnids (continental).

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Permian-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Krapovickas, et al., 2010)

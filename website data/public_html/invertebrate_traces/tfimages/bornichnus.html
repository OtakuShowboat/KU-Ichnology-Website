Bornichnus: Bromley and Uchman, 2003

DESCRIPTION: Small trace (mm scale) forming a tangle of lined tubes that are tortuously branched.

BEHAVIOR(S): Agrichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine; tidal flat

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Worm-like

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Different from Palaeophycus in its tangled morphology, abundant branching, and isolated patches.

Schaubcylindrichnus : Frey and Howard, 1981

DESCRIPTION: Vertically to sub horizontal oblong ellipsoidal well-lined sediment filled tube. Both interior and exterior wall surface is smooth. Can be singular tubes or occur in closely-spaced clusters.

BEHAVIOR(S): Dwelling or feeding burrow

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Common in shoreface settings, slightly seaward of true high-energy.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Marine suspension feeders or tubicolous deposit feeders such as maldanid polychaetes. Carnivorous foraminifera.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Miocene-Pleistocene.

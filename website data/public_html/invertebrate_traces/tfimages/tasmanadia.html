Tasmanadia : (Chapman, 1929)

DESCRIPTION: Double row of very sharp transverse imprints, commonly single but some joined to form bifid impressions.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine to terrestrial.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Arthropods

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Precambrian-recent.

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Emended: Glaessner, 1957)

REMARKS: Glaessner (1957) attributed the trace to arthropods.

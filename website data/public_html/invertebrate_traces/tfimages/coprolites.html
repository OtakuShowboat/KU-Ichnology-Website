Coprolites: Buckland, 1829

DESCRIPTION: Fossilized dung or excrement

BEHAVIOR(S): Waste produced by animals. Includes larger excrement, small fecal pellets, and microcoprolites. Can be used to interpret feeding behavior of animals, such as herbivores or carnivores.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine to continental. (Vast majority is continental)

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Any number of organisms producing excrement

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian to recent

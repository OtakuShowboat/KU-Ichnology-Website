Parawanichnus

DESCRIPTION: Interconnected nest of galleries and chambers.

BEHAVIOR(S): Multiple uses including dwelling, reproduction, and agriculture.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Multiple continental settings from eolian to alluvial. Presence of a indicates poorly- to well-developed soils above the water table.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Ants

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Jurassic-recent.

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Hasiotis, 2006)

Olivellites : Fenton and Fenton, 1937

DESCRIPTION: Horizontal, bilaterally symmetrical trail that have ribbon-like meniscate packages; may have a medial furrow.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia; Locomotion and feeding trail of a deposit feeder.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Shallow marine deposits.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Gastropods.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Olivellites is synonymized with Scolicia

Dictyodora: Weiss, 1884

DESCRIPTION: Meandering to spiral three-dimensional thin spreite burrow; exterior surface indistinctly striated; conical; burrow forms perpendicular to the bedding plane.

BEHAVIOR(S): Pascichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep-marine

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Worms or molluscs

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian-Carboniferous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Seilacher, 1955, 1967; Benton and Trewin, 1980; Benton, 1982

REMARKS: Description from Stachacz (2012)

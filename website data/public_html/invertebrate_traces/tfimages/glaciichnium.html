Glaciichnium: Walter, 1985

DESCRIPTION: Straight to winding trace consisting of sets of imprints. Imprints are straight to curved bars which are subparallel towards the trail axis and oblique towards the sides, appearing as sets of "V"s. May have a furrow along trail axis.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Lacustrine deposits.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Arthropods, isopods.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Eocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Uchman et al., 2009.

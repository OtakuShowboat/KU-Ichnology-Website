Mammillichuis: Chamberlain, 1971

DESCRIPTION: Small, circular to elliptical mounds with a central depression, which has a small tube at the depression’s center. The aperture of the tube might be concave or convex.

BEHAVIOR(S): Cubichnia or Domichnia; Hiding trace or end of burrow used by animal to work sediment for food or fecal pellets

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep marine flysch deposits, shallow marine, deltaic deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Worm?; Sea Anemone?

GEOLOGIC RANGE: ?Cambrian–recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Hantzschel, 1975; Uchman, 1998

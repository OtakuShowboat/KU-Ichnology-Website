Videos and Animations

Here we have compiled a variety of videos on ichnology. We have some videos of tracefossils, including some lectures and interviews provided by Dr. Hasiotis. There are videos showing modern animals creating structures that we observe in the fossil record as trace fossils. We also have videos showcasing the neoichnological research we have done here at KU, including real time burrowing experiments demonstrating the motion and behavior of organisms as they produce burrows, tracks, and trackways. Keep checking in as new videos are added.
